Please note:
Uses: In addition to the permitted and discretionary uses noted above, other uses, such as Home Occupations, Bed and Breakfast Homes and Inns, Public Parks etc. vary across all districts and no changes are proposed to those uses.
Max FAR: FAR means Floor Area Ratio which is the total floor area of all levels of a buildings on a site, divided by the area of the site. Through the current regulations, in order to build the maximum, specific conditions must be met such as providing parking underground and/or for specific housing types. The proposed changes would allow the maximum FAR when more homes are proposed.
Max Height: The maximum building height is calculated to the mid-point of the roof. For reference, 14.5 m allows for a 4 storey building.
Min Landscaping Requirement: The minimum landscaping requirement would include hard surfaced amenity areas as well as all soft landscaped areas, but wouldn't include driveways or surface parking areas.
Setbacks: The numbers above are the smallest permitted, however some of these increase as the building gets higher.
